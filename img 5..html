Onco-Life Cancer Centre

Onco-Life Cancer Centre a.k.a. Onco-Life Hospitals is the largest chain of Oncology Hospitals in Maharashtra. It was founded by Mr. Uday Deshmukh who has over 40+ years of extensive & comprehensive experience Nationally & Internationally in the field of Oncology. Onco-Life Cancer Centre was first established in Satara in 2014. We are now located in Satara, Chiplun, Talegaon & Wagholi.

Onco-Life Hospitals is considered as one of the topmost Oncology chains in India. As envisioned by Mr. Uday Deshmukh, Onco-Life Cancer Centre is a one stop Centre for all Personalized Cancer Diagnostics & Treatments. We strive to provide the best patient outcome with the help of advanced technologies & highly experienced medical professionals.

Equipped with 400+ beds all across, we are a state of the art Super Specialty Cancer Hospital with three NABH Accreditations awarded by ‘Quality Council of India’, an appropriate recognition for our high standards of quality healthcare as well as operational and management efficiency.

From Chairman’s Desk

Mr. Uday Deshmukh

Mr. Uday Deshmukh is the Founder & CEO of Onco-Life Hospitals. He is an Electronic Engineer who has more than 40+ years of experience in the field of Oncology. He has completed more than 50 Oncology Projects Worldwide. He has  completed several turnkey projects in Oncology for various well reputed hospitals. He is a well renowned National & International expert in Radiation Oncology.

Mr. Uday Deshmukh leads from the forefront the charismatic team of Onco-Life Hospitals. He plays a vital role in the overall growth and development of the organization. Being in the Oncology Field for the past 4 decades, he ensured of having the latest technologies for Onco-life Hospitals.

I have seen a huge gap in quality of treatment and services provided to patients in our country. I do empathize with what the patient & their loved ones go through during the entire process. Since I have vast knowledge, experience and International exposure, I decided to give up my job after giving a lot of thought and chose to go ahead with Onco-Life inspite of all risks & people advising me against it. Been working hard along with team ever since trying to be the best and a posterchild of a better change in Healthcare industry.
